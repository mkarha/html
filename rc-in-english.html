RC-Ratakeskus offers a multitude of fun activities to pass the time, from billiards and snookball to different types of tracks for racing with radio controlled cars. You can come drive with your own rc-car or rent one from RC-Ratakeskus.

Just visit us during opening hours Wednesday to Friday 15:00-21:00, Saturday and Sunday 12:00-21:00 without reservation.

RC-Ratakeskus is a great place to have birthday parties, company leisure events and other fun events, or just visit with friends for some friendly competitive fun or leisurely gaming.

We have a conference room that seats up to 20 people, which can be reserved. The conference room is equipped with audiovisual equipment and party games for those ”not so formal” events. You can also bring your own party snacks to the conference room and there is also a fridge to keep your refreshments cool.

If you want to book an event, you can reach us via e-mail info@rc-ratakeskus.com or by phone 044 977 2939. If you already have a location booked for your event, but there’s something missing check out our mobile drift track.
